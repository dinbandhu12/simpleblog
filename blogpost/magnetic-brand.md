## Strategy can feel quiet

A magnetic brand does not need to shout. It needs repetition, restraint, and a point of view that shows up the same way across the work.

Start by choosing the feeling you want people to remember. Then decide which visual choices support that feeling and which ones make the experience noisier.

## Keep the system small

- One primary type style for headlines.
- One practical type style for interface text.
- Two or three supporting colors.
- A consistent image crop.

Small systems are easier to repeat, and repetition is where trust begins.
